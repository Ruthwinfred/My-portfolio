About Me

Winners is a full stack developer specializing in both front and backend development. My career in web development started when I joined college to do ICT seven years ago. Three years later, I joined Moringa school for a one months coding bootcamp. That is when I learnt that a web developer is a full time learner because I realized that what I knew by then was not enough for my career. I therefore joined the Institute of siftware Technologies for a one year course in full stack development where I completed successfully and hence became a fulltime developer. Currently, I am a trainee at Emobilis academy and here, coding is so interesting.
